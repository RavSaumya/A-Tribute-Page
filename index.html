Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

Timeline of a Remarkable Life

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from Saint Joseph's College
  • 1960 - Joined DRDO as a scientist
  • 1969 - Transferred to ISRO
  • 1980 - Led Project SLV-III successfully
  • 1990 - Awarded Padma Bhushan
  • 2002-2007 - Served as the 11th President of India
  • 2015 - Passed away while doing what he loved - teaching students
